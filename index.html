Fluffy Pancake Recipe

Start your morning right with a stack of warm, fluffy pancakes made from scratch. With just a few simple ingredients, you'll whip up a delicious breakfast in no time. Whether topped with syrup, fresh fruits, or a dollop of butter, they're sure to please. Perfect for lazy weekends or special mornings. Pancakes bring comfort to every bite.

Preaparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 25 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter (plus more for the pan)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Combine wet ingredients: In another bowl, beat the egg, then add the milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract (if using). Mix well.
  3. Make the batter: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Stir gently until just combined. The batter should be slightly lumpy—avoid overmixing.
  4. Heat the pan: Preheat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease it with butter or oil.
  5. Cook the pancakes: Pour about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake onto the skillet. Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges look set (about 2-3 minutes), then flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm: Stack the pancakes on a plate and serve with your favorite toppings—maple syrup, fruit, whipped cream, or a pat of butter.
